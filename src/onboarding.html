Welcome to Sales Tax Gross-Up

Amazon.ca shows pre-tax prices while you browse — the sales tax only appears at the very end of checkout. This extension folds the tax back into the prices up front, so the number on screen reflects roughly what you'll actually pay.

Pick your province or territory below to get started. Prices stay unchanged until you choose, and you can change this any time from the extension icon.

This is an estimate for convenience, not an official tax calculation. It applies your province's combined sales-tax rate to every price. A small minority of items (some groceries, etc.) are tax-exempt or taxed differently — the extension doesn't try to carve those out, so it may show them as more expensive than they'll actually be at checkout. Everything happens locally in your browser — no data is collected.
